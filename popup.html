This website is an extension to fill in forms automaticaly







This website offers an intuitive browser extension that simplifies form-filling by automatically inputting saved information into online forms, saving users valuable time. Designed to streamline processes across various platforms, it securely stores data such as names, addresses, and payment details for quick, error-free submissions. The extension can detect required fields, ensuring accuracy and reducing manual effort. Ideal for anyone who frequently encounters repetitive forms, this tool enhances productivity while prioritizing privacy. With customizable profiles, users can switch between personal and professional data effortlessly, making it a powerful solution for efficient, safe, and seamless online interactions.
